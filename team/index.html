Friedrich Nietzsche

Professor of Philosophy

Friedrich is a German philosopher, cultural critic, composer, poet, and philologist whose work has exerted a profound influence on Western philosophy and intellectual history. He is best known for his philosophical works on morality, the will to power, and the concept of the Übermensch.
